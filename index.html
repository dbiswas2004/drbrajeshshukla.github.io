I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at GLA University, Mathura. My research focuses on the intersection of data science and healthcare, particularly in developing innovative solutions for elderly care and health monitoring.

My doctoral research at IIT Jodhpur involved developing the "Jodhpur Instrumented Kursi (JiK)" - an innovative instrumented chair designed to assist doctors in measuring the time of 5 Times Sit-to-Stand test and analyzing imbalanced geriatric data for sarcopenia prediction. This work represents a significant contribution to geriatric healthcare technology.

I have been privileged to work as a visiting researcher at the University of Bedfordshire, UK, under the Newton Bhabha Fellowship program, collaborating with international experts in health research and biomechanical engineering.
